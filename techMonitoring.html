Monitoring and Evaluation Support

The ThinkZone Support app assists Field Coordinators and Managers in supervising educators, monitoring children’s progress, getting real-time data to identify challenges faced by educators, and providing targeted support to educators based on those challenges. The app includes offline capabilities, scheduling, task management, and skill development modules. It is integrated with a centralized backend system for comprehensive monitoring and evaluation.
